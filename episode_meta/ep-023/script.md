**SWEET:** _(PHB_Silky, calm, at the stand)_ Ron came to me about a steady supply. I offered him something better. His own corner. A stand of his own. The Report reaches every settlement at once. That's not a corner, Ron. That's every corner.
» Sweet core, hand resting on the lemonade stand

**DR. GOLDTOOTH:** _(KattW, delighted)_ A NEWSMAN movin' weight on the AIR, baby?! That's GENIUS! He says "the story," everybody drinks! He says "developing," everybody drinks AGAIN! He's not reportin' the epidemic, baby — he IS the distribution! _(wiping a proud tear)_ Welcome to the practice, Ron.

**RON:** _(to camera, rationalizing hard)_ Now MACU, full transparency — and transparency is what I'm ABOUT — tonight's broadcast is brought to you by crystal. Pure crystal. From a name you trust — me. You trust me. You've trusted me for years. Nothing's changed. And if you've been on the fence, here's the news you can use: the first pour's on the house. The second one's a conversation.
» Ron core, holding a packet to camera like a press badge

**STRIDE:** _(HAL, warm)_ Engagement is up, Ron! Whatever you just did, do more of it. Hydration streak: six days. You're inspiring people. Eleven people have now matched your heart rate exactly. Synchronized. Lovely.

**RON:** _(infomercial cadence, sweating lightly)_ Are YOU tired, MACU? Thirsty? Haunted by something you did last week you can't quite name? Crystal doesn't fix that. But it makes the not-fixing taste like lemonade. Pour responsibly. Pour often. Pour now.
» Ron core, pitching at the stand → b-roll: crystal_pitcher being poured into a row of cups

**DARYL:** _(Spicoli, a satisfied customer testimonial, hollow-eyed)_ I heard about it on the NEWS, man. The news doesn't lie. Ron wouldn't lie. I trust Ron. I sold my house to be third in line. » Daryl core, beaming, swaying slightly

**WALTER:** _(serene, a human-interest tag)_ Daryl's been third in line, ninth in line, and, briefly, the line itself. We're proud of him.
» Walter core

**ROSCOE:** _(PHB_IceT, now managing the stand's line, Macho Man peak)_ The LINE is the LINE, brother! You cut the line at Ron's stand, you JOIN the product! The last man who cut in front is now SERVED in a CUP! We don't say which cup! Could be ANY cup! Drink up, MACU — OHHH YEAHHH!
» Roscoe core, wrangling a long orderly line at the stand
