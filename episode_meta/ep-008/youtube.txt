It's a quiet night in the MACU — no one has declared war since lunch, and the sky is only the normal amount of on fire. Ron and Walter bring you the Crater Ball semifinal (Sector Nine still hasn't lost a player in three games — there's a reason for that, and Ron does not recognize the scoreboard that says otherwise), a word from Norm at the Glow Plant, and a heartwarming community segment with a local man who recently won big on this very network. His prize: "Mr. Cricket Knows Where You Live." It has redecorated. It uses his name now.

Plus weather with Chip Pleasant, who — for the first time in the history of this broadcast — cannot call it mild. The moon is closer than it was this morning. The feed has been a touch glitchy. Probably the weather.
