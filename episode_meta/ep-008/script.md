## COLD OPEN — ANCHOR DESK

**RON:** Good evening, MACU. I'm Ron, and for once it's a quiet night. No one has declared war on us since
lunch, and the sky is only the normal amount of on fire.
» Ron core

**WALTER:** And I'm Walter. One small note before we begin: the feed's been a touch glitchy tonight.
Probably the weather. Nothing to worry about.
» Walter core → b-roll: static glitch (brief) [SFX: tv static blip]

**RON:** ...Right. The weather. I've been blaming the weather for eleven years, Walter. It has never once
been the weather.
» Ron core

**WALTER:** It's usually the weather.
» Walter core

**RON:** Tonight: it's Crater Ball, where Sector Nine has not lost a player in three games. A word from our
friend Norm at the Glow Plant. And we'll meet a local man who recently came into some good fortune.
» Ron core → MACU REPORT title card

## SEGMENT 1 — MACU SPORTS: CRATER BALL

am too excited about this. They are correct.
» Ron core → MACU SPORTS title card → b-roll: crater ball

**RON:** Sector Nine takes an early lead, and folks — what a season. Three games. Not a single player lost!

**WALTER:** Ron, the other teams have lost players.
» Walter core

**RON:** Sector Nine doesn't count those, Walter. Their rule is: it isn't a loss if you can't find the body.
It's a beautiful, beautiful game.

**WALTER:** Sector Eight lost four players to Sector Nine last week. It's on the board.
» Walter core

**RON:** I don't recognize that board. I've never seen that board in my life.
» Ron core

**WALTER:** It's the only board, Ron.
» Walter core

**RON:** Speaking of good fortune — Walter, remind the people at home how we define that around here.
» Ron core

## SEGMENT 2 — PERSON ON THE STREET: A LOCAL WINNER

**WALTER:** For our community segment, we found a local man who recently won big right here on this
network. Sir — congratulations.
» Walter core

**GERALD:** Thanks. Yeah. I won the home edition of Wheel of Misfortune. My prize was "Mr. Cricket Knows
Where You Live."
» Gerald core → b-roll: settlement street

**WALTER:** Wonderful. And how has the prize changed your life?
» Walter core

**GERALD:** He comes Tuesdays. He's usually already inside when I get home. He's redecorated. And he uses my
name now — which is strange, because I never told him my name.
» Gerald core → b-roll: settlement street

**GERALD:** He's been teaching my kids a little song. (beat) I don't have kids.
» Gerald core → b-roll: settlement street

**RON:** Walter, that man is being hunted by a puppet and we are calling it "good fortune." Does that not
strike anyone else as—
» Ron core

**WALTER:** Weather.
» Walter core
