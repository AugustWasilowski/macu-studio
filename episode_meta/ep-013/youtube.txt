DESCRIPTION

The Road to the Crater Bowl, Day Three. The Sector Nine Slags' roster stands at zero — the last living player walked into the wasteland during last night's weather — so Ron does the only responsible thing and holds the first-ever live Crater Bowl tryout.

Coach Bodhi judges the candidates on energy, finds the living a little too afraid to compete, and builds his squad around a steel drum, a strong silent fella in a trench coat, and a promising empty space by the third cone. When that doesn't pan out, Ron signs the only frame with real heart left in the building: Brenda — co-anchor Walter's car, current residence of Gary from the weekend shift. She starts at left tackle.

Plus a word from Survival Strength Sanctum, a Crater Bowl line the bookmakers refuse to price, and the Glowboys posting a forty-yard dash time that is, in Crater Carl's words, "a new record, and also possibly a sin."

The MACU Report is a black-and-white, post-apocalyptic, retro-futurist faux-newscast from the Mayor Awesome Cinematic Universe. It's a young team. It's a brave team. It has heart. Good night, and good luck.
