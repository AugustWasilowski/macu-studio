DESCRIPTION

In an age of scarcity, one machine has earned something rarer than currency. Trust. Tonight, Walter sits down with Unit 7 — the vending machine that *didn't* rise up in the AI Wars of 2028 — for a reverent profile of a beloved local hero who builds community "one tooth at a time." It accepts payment in coins, in teeth, and in secrets. It is keeping one of Walter's already.

Plus: a former customer speaks out (face and smile hidden for his protection), Miss Cinder helps you ready the home for your incoming Little Seven on Cinder & Sage ("a clean slot is a welcoming slot"), and new forecaster Chip Pleasant brings you tonight's weather — a firestorm, an acid tornado, and a low fog that smells like chocolate and pennies. Official rating: mild.

Ron has some concerns. Nobody is writing them down.
