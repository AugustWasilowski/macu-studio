DESCRIPTION
-----------
The eve of the Crater Bowl. With the Sector Nine Slags' roster down to a car, a barrel, and a respectfully observed empty space, Ron files the final paperwork and activates the only asset he has left: himself. Coach Bodhi blesses it — a lifetime of sitting perfectly still under a hot light is, it turns out, ideal crater preparation. "You will not feel a thing. That is the goal."

Then it's the team's first and only practice (the barrel is doing laps and leads the roster in game-reps), a word from Bobby's Outpost ("refurbished Phantom Rovers, runs great, barely haunted"), and the Glowboys' final session — six hours of standing perfectly still and glowing, which their coordinator calls transcendent preparation and Coach Bodhi calls plagiarism.

Tomorrow: the Crater Bowl. A car, a coach at peace, a barrel somewhere in the dark, and Ron — who intends to play in the game and host the broadcast at the same time. They have not solved how.

The MACU Report is a black-and-white, post-apocalyptic, retro-futurist faux-newscast from the Mayor Awesome Cinematic Universe. The weather has run out of warnings. Good night, and good luck.
