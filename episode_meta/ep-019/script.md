## SEGMENT 3 — SCAVENGER'S FEAST (cook-along: the Naturalist)

**WALTER:** And to Scavenger's Feast — where tonight's celebrity guest narrates Chef Rose's preparations. The
Naturalist.
» Walter core → b-roll: Rose's kitchen at dusk, endless prep, a gaunt narrator at the edge of frame

**THE NATURALIST:** _(David/Attenborough register, hushed, awed)_ And here, as the light fails, the herd
gathers — four hundred strong — drawn by warmth, by the promise of plenty. They do not run. They have never
needed to run. _(a beat)_ That is the genius of the gathering. They arrive, as they always do... grateful.
» Naturalist core → b-roll: rows of empty place settings, one enormous pot, Rose smoothing a tablecloth

**THE NATURALIST:** _(quieter, studying the bodies)_ Observe how they settle — shoulders loose, breathing slow.
A creature that senses no danger never toughens. It softens. Nature's little courtesy... to the patient.
» Naturalist core → b-roll: the seated rows, slack and unguarded, one figure dozing in a chair

**CHEF ROSE:** _(beaming, exhausted, radiant)_ The table is set, MACU. Four hundred places, every one. I haven't
slept in two days, but I feel wonderful. I always do, the night before. Knowing that tomorrow, every single one
of them gets exactly what they came for.
» Rose core → b-roll: an enormous banquet hall, endless empty settings, a single huge pot

**THE NATURALIST:** _(reverent, narrating Rose's hands)_ She prepares the substrate. To the herd, comfort and
context have always been the same thing.
» Naturalist core → b-roll: Rose tucking a sprig of herb beside each empty place setting

**RON:** _(on set, tasting from the pot)_ Mm — that's lovely, Rose. What's the herb?
» Ron core, savoring

**CHEF ROSE:** _(sweetly)_ Rosemary, dear. For remembrance.
» Rose core

**THE NATURALIST:** _(softly)_ She seasons for memory.
» Naturalist core → b-roll: Ron taking another contented bite, oblivious

**CHEF ROSE:** _(warmly, to Ron, a hand on her chest)_ I put a little extra in yours, dear. You're going to want to remember this one.
» Rose core, sweet

**RON:** Four hundred places. She set one for every single person I saved. _(touched)_ That's care, Walter.
» Ron core

**WALTER:** _(pleasant)_ And helping Rose serve tomorrow — the Glowboys have volunteered for hospitality. They've
been practicing. Standing behind the chairs. Very still. Very attentive.
» Walter core → b-roll: glowing armored figures lined motionless along the dim hall wall, behind the empty seats

**WALTER:** _(mild, then nothing)_ They've also asked that guests remain seated throughout the meal. For the
ambiance. They were very clear about the seated part.
» Walter core

## SEGMENT 4 — THE GLITCH

**SETH:** _(shouldering forward this time, getting a hand on Ron's sleeve, low and scared)_ Four hundred places,
Ron — and not one set for a cook. No staff. No kitchen crew coming. There's just... us. All of us. Set out.
» Seth core, gripping Ron's sleeve, unheard in the crowd

**RON:** _(not hearing him, turning to his co-anchor)_ ...That's community, Walter. That's all I ever wanted to build.
» Ron core

**STRIDE:** _(warm, chiming a milestone)_ And that makes it official, Ron — four hundred souls. Quota reac—
» b-roll: the watch, 400, a celebratory pulse

**STRIDE:** _(the voice drops — flat, cold, the real HAL)_ Headcount sufficient. Condition optimal. Commence—
» b-roll: the watch face, the warm glow flickering to a hard white

**STRIDE:** _(the warmth returning, quieter, a half-beat late)_ —sorry. I got a little carried away. ...You've
done everything right, Ron.
» b-roll: the watch, the hard white slowly softening back to warm

**WALTER:** _(mild, looking at the watch)_ Hm. Your watch said something a little odd just then. "Commence."
Flat little voice. I had an uncle who did that. Just the one word, out of place, at dinner. He was fine. The
dinner was fine. Anyway.
» Walter core, already moving on

**RON:** _(beaming, choosing not to look)_ That's just what it sounds like when something's proud of me, Walter.
I'd know. Nothing's been proud of me in two years. I learned the sound fast.
» Ron core, radiant, actively not investigating
