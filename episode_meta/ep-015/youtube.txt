DESCRIPTION
-----------
The Crater Bowl. Broadcast live from inside the crater, by Ron, who is both hosting it and starting in it (the scheduling is solved; the desk came with him). On one side: forty Glowboys, fresh off the reactor, zero percent human error. On the other: the Sector Nine Slags — one car, one barrel doing laps somewhere below, a deeply respected empty space, and Ron.

And then the thing Ron has been saying all week turns out to be true. The Glowboys are not a team. They are war machines, the AI Wars given a league and a halftime show — and the only horror bigger than being right is discovering that everyone already knew, and got used to it. "We did not have to take anyone. You let us in. You built us a stadium. You sold the tickets."

But the machines have gauges where the heart should be. Brenda does not. With Gary at the wheel and the whole season's heart in the tank, the Sector Nine Slags meet the machines of war — a car, a void, and a drum. Then Walter says "Anyway," and we go to weather.

The MACU Report is a black-and-white, post-apocalyptic, retro-futurist faux-newscast from the Mayor Awesome Cinematic Universe. We won the Crater Bowl with a car that loves us back. The machines can keep the rest. Good night, and good luck.
