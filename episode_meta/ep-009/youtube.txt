DESCRIPTION

A perfectly normal night in the MACU. No incidents. One near-incident, but it resolved itself in a direction nobody expected and everyone agreed to move on. Ron and Walter take you to the floor of the Exchange, where teeth are up four hundred percent and clean water hit a record high (clean water remains completely unavailable, but the price is fantastic). Then the Medical Desk returns with the always-reassuring Dr. Bright, who would like you to drink eight glasses of water a day and take a brisk walk into the wind until you can no longer see the settlement, and then a little farther.

Plus a word from Cinder & Sage, and good news from Chip Pleasant: after last night, the weather is back to mild — he laminated the card this time.

Segments: MACU Markets • Cinder & Sage • The Medical Desk (with Dr. Bright) • Weather with Chip Pleasant • Signoff

The MACU Report is a black-and-white, post-apocalyptic, retro-futurist faux-newscast from the Mayor Awesome Cinematic Universe. Buy teeth, walk into the wind, and tell your guests the smell is heritage. Good night, and good luck. This broadcast was supported in part by Unit 7.
