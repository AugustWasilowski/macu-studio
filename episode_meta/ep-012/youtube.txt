DESCRIPTION
-----------
The Road to the Crater Bowl, Day Two. Tonight we scout the opposition — the Glowboys, forty men powered direct from the Glow Plant reactor, every one of them aglow, none of them tired, and (legally) still people. Mostly. Norm from Nuclear is delighted to walk you through it.

Back at the desk, the Sector Nine Slags are down to a single living player, so Ron does the only responsible thing: he benches his last man for his own safety — over the serene objections of Coach Bodhi, who feels that letting him play, and relocate his energy, is the kindest thing a roster can do. The player, for his part, would like to go home. He gets his wish, quietly, during the weather.

Plus a word from Phoenix Apothecary (a mere weed becomes a lifeline), the updated Crater Bowl line, and Gary from the weekend shift, who has found Walter's car in the lot and named her Brenda. They're very happy out there.

The MACU Report is a black-and-white, post-apocalyptic, retro-futurist faux-newscast from the Mayor Awesome Cinematic Universe. Two days out. One Slag, forty glowing men, and all the heart in the world. Good night, and good luck.
