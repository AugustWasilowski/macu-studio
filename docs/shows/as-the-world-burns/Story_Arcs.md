## Continuity rules / canon facts

- There is **no clean water, ever.** Lake Mirabel is a clay pit; the family admires the clay sincerely.
- **The Product is never explained** and never winked at. It is treated as sacred; "the Product," "the
  Pitcher," "a pour," "the reserves," "the accords" are all said with funereal gravity.
- **Nobody acknowledges the world ended.** They summer at the lake. They have brunch.
- The **Resurrection Engine never speeds up or gets lampshaded** — each return is stated flat and dropped.
- **Sheldon is canon-smart.** Once Brick becomes Sheldon, Sheldon quietly out-thinks the room.
